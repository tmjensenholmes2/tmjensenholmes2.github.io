Jamal Evans and Jeremy Holmes, Proprietors

About

Alliance Barbershop and Supply is a traditional barbershop located in the Park East Neighborhood of Omaha, Nebraska. Owners Jamal Evans and Jeremy Holmes have an unrivaled passion for barbering with 10 plus years in the industry.

Offering exceptional haircuts, straight razor shaves, eard trims w/straight edge and banter.

Jamal

Jamal found his passion for barbering in early 2006 while playing collegiate basketball. After traditional secondary eduction, Jamal attended College of Hair Design in Lincoln, NE. Jamal co-founded Alliance Barbershop and Supply in 2016. He excels in every aspect of the barber industry. Jamal is always looking for opportunities to grow on a personal and professional level.

Jeremy

Jeremy found his passion for barbering at his grandfather's funeral. Jerry Holmes and his father, Bert Holmes were barbers and barbershop owners since the early 1900's. Jeremy attended Joseph's BarberColege in Omaha, NE; Capitol School of Hairstyling in Omaha, NE and College of Hair Design in Lincoln, NE. Jeremy co-founded Alliance Barbershop and Supply in 2016. He excels in every aspect of the barber industry. Jeremy is always looking for opportunities to grow on a personal and professional level.
